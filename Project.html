Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter

Recipe

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta with the garlic until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then add the grated cheeses and mix well.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta, removing the garlic.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
